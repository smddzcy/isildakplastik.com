STORBOX BLINDS

General Features

Egepen Deceuninck Storbox Blinds System is not only a practical and aesthetical solution for your home, but also provides an excellent level of insulation. Banish outside noise and weather while keeping a peaceful indoor sanctuary.

It is possible to carry out different applications with the Storbox Blinds System produced in two different sizes: 165 mm and 200 mm depending on the details. In addition, there are boxes of 165x200 mm and 200x235 in the system for heat insulation. It is also possible to apply Ege (55 mm) and Mini (37 mm) blinds. This system is easy to apply on a single window, door or such locations requiring multi blinds. In addition, it may be combined with a slide insect net or blind insect net.

SHUTTERS

General Features

Our culture of architectural excellence drives the designs that suit every project and taste from nostalgic to modern.

Combining the aesthetic with functionality is the harmonious key towards our stunning design options for your home. systems designed for your houses.

The inner flanks of our shutter system could be opened forwardly with a special clipper system. The shutters are divided into two groups depending on the installation detail: wall mounting and frame mounting. You may choose the colors of white and laminated options for the shutter systems. It is possible to produce arched shutter systems depending on the location.

This shutter system patented by Ege Profil Tic. ve San. A.Ş. is compatible with every PVC window systems and, used for years with confidence.

DECORATIVE WAINSCOT

General Features

Wainscots easily applicable to outdoors and indoors are designed as an elegant, eye-pleasing insulation material for your walls.

The wide wainscot group of 250 mm and 200 mm, the grooved wainscot types of 125 mm and 200 mm are designed to meet the requirements of different locations.

Efficient solutions are provided due to the wainscot edges, interior and exterior edge turns, decorative profiles and special details. Thanks to the pattern printed wainscots used in the indoor decorations, you are free to create a difference in your locations. It is possible to apply all laminated board color options available in the system on a wide laminated board of 200 mm that fit the outdoors and indoors.

INSECT SCREEN

General Features

An insect screen is the most effective solution for fighting insects without insecticide. It allows you to open your windows at any time to ventilate your room. It is possible to apply a sliding insect screen or inward opening insect screen to suit your type of window. Installation is easy and suitable for fixing to any material, i.e. wood, aluminum or pvc. Our full colour range of laminates is available.
